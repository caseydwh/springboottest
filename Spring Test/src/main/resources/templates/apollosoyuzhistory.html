The Apollo Soyuz Test Project

The Apollo Soyuz Test Project was the first major international cooperation in space projects between the United States and the Soviet Union in 1975. On July 15, 1975, an Apollo spacecraft launched carrying a crew of three and docked with a Soyuz spacecraft on July 17th.
